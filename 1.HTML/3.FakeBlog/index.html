Lebron James

Lebron James is a basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers. Over his career, he has amassed:

  • 3 NBA Championships
  • 14x NBA All-Star
  • 4 NBA Finals MVPs
  • 9 Trips to the NBA Finals (8 consecutive)
